## Description

**Discover the hidden patterns in your emotional life.**

PulseMac reads the quiet signals in your daily life — how you spend your time, what you write, how your mood connects to your routines — and surfaces patterns you never knew existed. When are you happiest? Most energized? Most at peace?

It's not another productivity tool or a mood-tracker you have to maintain. Pulse quietly observes, learns, and reveals. Think of it as a personal emotional cartographer that maps your inner world over time.

**How it works:**
- **Capture moments** — Snap a photo, record a voice note, or jot a journal entry. Pulse reads between the lines.
- **See your patterns** — Weekly insights reveal what triggers your best days and hardest ones.
- **Follow your mood chains** — Watch how emotions ripple across days and weeks.
- **100% Private** — Everything stays on your Mac. No cloud, no servers, no data collection.

**Who it's for:**
- People curious about their emotional patterns
- Anyone who wants to understand what makes their days feel good or difficult
- People who prefer privacy-first tools that don't harvest their data

## Mental Health Disclaimer

> **Important:** PulseMac is a self-awareness and emotional insight tool, not a mental health diagnostic or treatment tool. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing persistent feelings of depression, anxiety, or emotional distress, please consult a qualified mental health professional. PulseMac does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

This disclaimer is shown:
- On first launch (setup screen)
- In the app's Settings → About section
