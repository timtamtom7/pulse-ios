## Technical Specs

| Device | Resolution | PPI | Notes |

| Mac Desktop (6K) | 5120 × 2880 | 218 | Pro Display XDR reference |

- Format: PNG (lossless)
- Color space: Display P3 (wide color)
- Light mode AND dark mode required for all 5 shots
- Total: 10 images (5 shots × 2 appearances)
- Naming convention: `MacBookPro14_Light_01.png`, `MacBookPro14_Dark_01.png`, etc.
